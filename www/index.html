About Us

Nina grew up in California with her loving parents, grandparents, and younger sister. She has always held a passion for math and science. After getting her electrical engineering and computer science degree, she started working for Google in 2014.

Andrew grew up in England, close to Nottingham (which you may know from Robin Hood). From a young age, he always enjoyed working on various software and mechanical projects, inside and outside of university / work. He moved to the U.S. in 2010, and joined Google in 2014.

The couple met in 2019, at Google. Thanks to many shared interests in tech, they grew to become close friends over the years, working on various projects, playing games, hiking etc. This very naturally and organically grew into something more, and the rest is history.
